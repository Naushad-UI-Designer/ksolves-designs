Overview

For businesses who want to ensure their shoppers reach the finish line as quickly as possible, the Magento 2 One-Step Checkout extension is your one-stop solution. It allows store admins to fully convert multiple checkout pages in a single page. Optimize the checkout process on the frontend by changing the layout, themes, and colors, and removing all factors leading to cart abandonment. With advanced options such as choosing from different layouts for easy navigation, Google auto-address suggestions, adding gift messages, billing & shipping information on one page, you can convert every visitor into a paying customer.

Reduce Cart Abandonment

In 2019, the Baymard Institute found that the average cart abandonment rate was 69.57%. Cart abandonment is a challenge that every ecommerce business faces, as it heavily correlates with customer conversion rates and revenue. Here's how the One Step Checkout eliminates the friction in a Magento 2 default checkout process.

All Features

Optimized Checkout Page

By bringing the long step checkout processes on one page and adding features such as Google Address Suggestion, 3 Column layout, etc., the Magento 2 One Step Checkout Extension simplifies the shopping process.

Customizable Checkout Theme

A store administrator can easily customize the appearance of the checkout page with this module. There are three themes available - dark, light, and default.

Choose Your Page Layout

There are three options for the admin to customize the layouts. They can showcase all checkout fields under 1,2 and 3 column layouts.

Move/Drag Checkout Fields

The checkout page customization has become much easier than ever, just set the order of the checkout fields by rearranging them.

Order Comments for Personalized User Experience

Users can add individual comments and remarks to their orders before checking out by enabling order comments at the checkout process.

Newsletter Subscription

With Newsletter Subscription enabled on the checkout page, your business is able to communicate with new customers and enhance its authority and credibility.

Skip Time Consuming Address Entry

It can fill out the user's location automatically based on their IP address, thanks to the GEO IP feature and Google Address suggestion.

Guests Can Checkout In A Click

Make the checkout process more convenient for guests by allowing them to create an account while doing the checkout. Easy Navigation For “Back To Store” Link Using “back to store” link, users can navigate between cart and store easily and add more items to their shopping cart on the fly.

Customisable Shipping And Billing Section

Through drag-and-drop functionality in Magento 2 One-Step Checkout extension, admin can easily customize the Fields selection & design layouts of the shipping and billing section.

Edit Product Options

Every feature of the One-Step Checkout Extension is optimized to speed up the checkout process of ecommerce stores. On the checkout page, customers can change color, size, and other attributes of items in their cart.

Seal Block

If the Seal blocks are enabled, admins can display trust badges from their third-party service providers to demonstrate their Magento 2 store's legitimacy and gain customer trust.

Static Block

If the Seal blocks are enabled, admins can display trust badges from their third-party service providers to demonstrate their Magento 2 store's legitimacy and gain customer trust.

Give A Intuitive Title To Your Checkout Page

The Magento 2 One Step Checkout allows store admin to change the title of the checkout page. You can, for example, replace "Checkout Page" with "Complete Your Purchase" or anything else you prefer.

One-Click Checkout

With one click, your customers can add products to the cart and be seamlessly redirected to the checkout page

Compatible With Desirable Payment Options.

It doesn't matter if it is PayPal, Authorize.Net, Braintree, Stripe, Amazon Pay option — The Magento 2 One Step Checkout Extension offers support for all payment gateways that are most convenient to users.
